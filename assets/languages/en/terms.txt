<h1 style='color: #D48166'>Terms of Use</h1>
<br>
<p>This set of rules was last updated on <b>22-05-2022</b> (dd-mm-YYYY) and applies to all discord servers that follow the
    are part of the Nevar network.
    We reserve the right to adapt and change the following rules at any time without prior notice.
</p>
<br>
<h2 style='color: #D48166'>Explanations of terms</h2>
<li>User: Any independent account, regardless of its true identity, is referred to as a user.</li>
<li>Channel hopping: Channel hopping is the constant switching from one voice channel to another.</li>
<br>
<h2 style='color: #D48166'>§1 Obligations of a User</h2>
<h4 style='color: #D48166'>§1.1 Compliance with the rules</h4>
<p>(1) Every user is bound by the rules and obliged to follow them.</p>
<br>
<h4 style='color: #D48166'>§1.2 Acknowledgment of the regulations</h4>
<p>(1) Every user accepts the set of rules by entering the server or by inviting the bot. He confirms that he has
    observed the rules in full and will abide by them.
</p>
<p>(2) There is no right of cancellation of the rules, only the user leaves the server or kicks the bot.</p>
<p>(3) Every user is obliged to independently inform themselves about the updates and changes to the set of rules.
    Ignorance in no way protects against punishment.
</p>
<br>
<h4 style='color: #D48166'>§1.3 Obligation to report</h4>
<p>(1) Each user is subject to a reporting obligation. If a user (passively or actively) detects a violation of the
    rules, he is obliged to report this to a team member as quickly as possible.
</p>
<br>
<h4 style='color: #D48166'>§1.4 Follow-up performance by moderators</h4>
<p>(1) Every user is obliged to follow requests from moderators as long as they do not do it themselves
    violate rules or moral principles.
</p>
<br>
<h4 style='color: #D48166'>§1.5 Account representation</h4>
<p>(1) Profile pictures, nicknames, "About me" and everything else must not be infringing, offensive, prohibited or illegal
    contain protected components and symbols.
</p>
<p>(2) Likewise, the above must not be copied by another user in order to be able to impersonate them.</p>
<br>
<h4 style='color: #D48166'>§1.6 General guidelines</h4>
<p>(1) It is mandatory to permanently adhere to the
    <a rel='noopener' target='_blank' href='https://discord.com/terms'>Discord Terms of Service</a>,
    the <a rel='noopener ' target='_blank' href='https://discord.com/guidelines'>Discord Community Guidelines</a> and
    to comply with German law.
</p>
<br>
<h2 style='color: #D48166'>§2 Behavior in chat</h2>
<h4 style='color: #D48166'>§2.1 Tone of voice</h4>
<p>(1) Friendliness and courtesy always have top priority. Every user has to behave in such a way that no
    other user feels harassed.
</p>
<p>(2) Insults, provocative discussions and any form of spam are to be avoided.</p>
<br>
<h4 style='color: #D48166'>§2.2 Distribution of Content</h4>
<p>(1) Assert and glorify or disseminate any form of material related to violence, sexism, racism
    or other immoral topics will not be tolerated in any way.
</p>
<p>(2) False statements, slander or similar are prohibited.</p>
<p>(3) Private data such as telephone numbers, addresses, pictures, etc. must not be publicly requested or
    be replaced. Account sharing by passing on passwords is also prohibited.
</p>
<p>(4) External advertising of any kind in image and writing, including private messages to users of our server, is prohibited.</p>
<p>(5) Messages and pictures should always fit the given topic of the chat.</p>
<br>
<h2 style='color: #D48166'>§3 Behavior in the voice channel</h2>
<h4 style='color: #D48166'>§3.1 Personal rights</h4>
<p>(1) Under no circumstances may the voice, camera or live transmission of other users be recorded without their express consent.</p>
<br>
<h4 style='color: #D48166'>§3.2 Channel hopping</h4>
<p>(1) Channel hopping is prohibited.</p>
<br>
<h2 style='color: #D48166'>§4 Use of the Nevar Bot</h2>
<h4 style='color: #D48166'>§4.1 Minimum age</h4>
<p>(1) We are always ready to consistently enforce the minimum age specified by Discord. You can find out which minimum age applies to you here:
    <a rel='noopener' target='_blank' href='https://support.discord.com/hc/en/articles/360040724612'>Why is Discord asking for my birthday?</a>
</p>
<br>
<h4 style='color: #D48166'>§4.2 Bug Abuse</h4>
<p>(1) It is forbidden to spam commands to overload the bot.</p>
<p>(2) Abusing bugs for your own benefit/to the detriment of others is prohibited.</p>
<p>(3) In the case of bugs that occur, §§1 3 Para. 1 also applies: Every user is obliged to report any bugs found to a team member immediately.</p>
<br>
<h4 style='color: #D48166'>§4.3 Liability</h4>
<p>(1) We are not liable for any content sent and distributed by a user via the Nevar Bot (e.g. via embed command).
    We can show at any time which user is responsible for which content.
</p>
<br>
<h2 style='color: #D48166'>§5 Our approach to rule violations</h2>
<h4 style='color: #D48166'>§5.1 Basic penalty</h4>
<p>(1) In principle, every violation of the rules is dealt with individually. We try to stick to the principle
    of proportionality.
</p>
<br>
<h4 style='color: #D48166'>§5.2 Rule violations on the support server</h4>
<p>(1) If there are rule violations on the support server, the result can be anything from a timeout to a permanent ban.
    The team has certain guidelines on how to respond, but the exact punishment may vary from time to time.
</p>
<br>
<h4 style='color: #D48166'>§5.3 Rule violations when using the bot</h4>
<p>(1) If there are rule violations when using Nevar, the bot will in most cases temporarily block the user.
    However, this can also be permanent under certain circumstances.
</p>
